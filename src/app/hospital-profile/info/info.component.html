About me:

The orthopedic care with compassion. Dr. Jawad's team comprises of dedicated Doctors, Operation theater staff, Physiotherapists & support staff who work in tandem & whose respective services complement each other for the total recovery & satisfaction of the patients. 24hrs Ambulance service is provided free of cost. The Hospital has a cashless facility for all insurance health cards & state govt employees. The aarogyasree facility is available.

Beyond the operating room, I am committed to educating my patients about their conditions, empowering them to actively participate in their own healing process. By fostering a collaborative relationship, I aim to ensure that every patient feels heard, supported, and well-informed throughout their orthopedic journey.
